Reservation Policy

Reservations are subject to availability and confirmation. A confirmed reservation ensures your table for 30 minutes after the scheduled time. If you are late beyond this period, your table may be released. Walk-ins are accepted based on table availability.


Cancellation & No-Show Policy

Cancellations must be made at least 4 hours in advance. Failure to show up without prior notice may result in restrictions on future reservations.


Group Bookings & Special Requests

For reservations of 10 or more guests, a deposit may be required. Special requests (seating preferences, dietary requirements, etc.) will be accommodated where possible but are not guaranteed.


Time Limits & Dining Duration

Reservations are allotted a dining time of 4 hours. Extensions may be accommodated if space permits.


Changes to Reservation

Any modifications to your reservation (date, time, number of guests) should be communicated as early as possible.


Conduct & House Rules

We reserve the right to refuse service to guests who violate house rules, exhibit inappropriate behavior, or exceed the reserved seating capacity. Outside food and drinks are not permitted unless previously authorized.


Force Majeure

The restaurant reserves the right to cancel or modify reservations due to unforeseen circumstances, including but not limited to natural disasters, power outages, or emergencies.


By making a reservation, you agree to these terms and conditions.
